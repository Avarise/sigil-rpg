### Character Creation
    This systems uses classless progression tree,
    which split skills into three disciplines: Cunning, Mysticism, Prowess.
    Characters Archetypes serve as example character builds. 
    
    Player character must be one of the Core Nation species: 
    Elphar Alliance (Elph) 
    Sildorian Commonwealth (Sildorian)
    Miris Hive Mind (Awakened Miris Drone)

    At level 1, character gets 2 Discipline points and additional points based on species. 
    Now that you know how many discipline points you have, allocate them. You may leave points unspent for later if you wish to do so. 

    To unlock a discipline, you need to spend 2 DP, for each tier you unlock. After you have unlocked a given tier, you are always granted Innate benefits of that discipline tier. One common Innate benefit among all disciplines is the Attribute Score Improvement (ASI). 

    To unlock further features and abilities, you need to spend 1 DP per ability. 

    At creation, character is granted Standard Array [15, 14, 13, 12, 10, 8] of Attributes. You may assign those to as you see fit. Remember to apply ASIs from your disciplines innate bonus. 

    After you chose your species, disciplines and attributes, it is time to allocate your Skills. There is 20 skills in the game. Number of skills a character gets at start is based on the species. Additionally, you gain more or less skills based on your intelligence modifiers. A character may spend 1 skill point to gain proficiency or 2 skill points to gain expertise. 

    Now, that you are almost at the end of the process, you need to choose your Origin (Backstory) and Gear. 
    Fill you character sheet and calculate other statistics such as AC, HP.

### Levels
    Character levels range from 1 to 20. With each level up, characters statistics 
    grow, increasing their proficiency, number of disciplines known, total hit points 
    as well as focus, which is a measure of how much actions a character can perform in 
    a given round. 

    Characters meant to be more powerful, may obtain Legendary Levels, up to 20. 
    Each legendary level grants +1 discipline point and +1 to attribute score improvement. 
    This is an optional reward that disregards combat difficulty and game balance.

### Disciplines

    Disciplines provide a way for a character to specialize and develop unusual abilities. 
    Split into three subsections: Cunning, Mysticism, Prowess, each of them focuses on different features. 
    They come in four tiers, each other tier available to higher level characters. 

    Tier I: character level 1.
    Tier II: character level 5.
    Tier III: character level 10.
    Tier IV: character level 15. 

    Progressing a character:  
    Unlocking a tier of discipline costs 2 Discipline Points.  
    Upon unlocking you are granted innate features of this discipline. 
    Unlocking other features, such as auras, stances, or abilities, costs 1 Discipline point. 

    Core Abilities: 
    Abilities that come in four stages, each available with their respective discipline tier. 
    Each character should invest into at least one of these abilities, and put their DP as soon as possible. 

    Stances: 
    Whenever you start your turn, you may enter a chosen stance.
    Only one stance can be active and this limit cannot be exceeded. 
    Stances end when you are incapacitated or unconscious.

    Auras: 
    Whenever you finish a short or a long rest, you can activate one of your auras. 
    They persist as long as you are conscious. 

### Attributes
    Each character is granted Attribute Array [15, 14, 13, 12, 10, 8] which may be
    assigned in any combination to six character Attributes. These may be further 
    improved with ASI - Attribute Score Improvements, which are granted when a
    character unlocks a tier of Discipline. 

    Attributes may be improved up to 20.  
    To increase attribute beyond 20, first, a character has to reach 
    level 10 and have at least two attributes increased to 20. 

    Strength: carry weight, melee combat, jumping distance 
    Dexterity: initiative, reflexes, ranged weapons 
    Constitution: hit points, condition resistance, meta powers, bio-attacks 
    Intelligence: skill points, logic, spellcasting 
    Wisdom: intuition, vision, willpower, spellcasting 
    Charisma: influence, luck, spellcasting, personality and impact on the world
