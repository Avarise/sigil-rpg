### Skill Training
    Skills represent knowledge and ability of an individual,
    specialization in certain tasks, or natural talent and proclivities towards them. 

    Character Skills are modified by associated Attribute modifier,
    and depending on level of training, by character's proficiency modifier: 
    0 - Unskilled   - no proficiency modifier  
    1 - Proficient  - adds proficiency modifier 
    2 - Expertise   - adds proficiency modifier twice

    Number of skill points a character gets is based on species, as well as on intelligence modifier. 
    Expertise requires at least level 5 or intelligence score 15 or higher. 
    Some skills, have additional requirements for training. 

### List of Skills
    Arcana (Intelligence) 
    Knowledge of Mysticism, forces, alternate dimensions, and beings that 
    dwell in them. 

    Athletics (Strength) 
    Feats of practiced strength, jumping, running, swimming, carrying heavy 
    objects. 

    Biology (Intelligence or Wisdom) 
    Practice of rules that define well known life in universe. Identification 
    of sicknesses and wounds, treating ailments. 

    Deception (Charisma) 
    Ability to lie effectively without rising any concerns. 

    Empathy (Wisdom) 
    Ability to read feelings and intentions of a given entity or creature. 

    Engineering (Intelligence) 
    Knowledge of various laws and practices in fields of engineering, physics 
    and mathematics. 

    Fortitude (Constitution) 
    Avoidance of effects that directly affect your body, like poisons and 
    diseases.  
    Requires Prowess I for proficiency and Prowess IV for expertise. 

    History (Intelligence) 
    Knowledge of past events, civilizations and places. 

    Intimidation (Charisma or Strength) 
    Art of acting upon target's fears, and forcing them to take a certain decision. 

    Investigation (Intelligence) 
    Ability to look for clues and hints in order to solve a problem. 

    Navigation (Wisdom) 
    Efficient pathfinding, knowledge of landmarks and stellar objects. 

    Perception (Wisdom) 
    Ability to discern patterns and spot things with just a glance.  

    Performance (Charisma or Constitution) 
    A wide group of skills and abilities that include performing art and services, 
    such as playing music, serving guests. 

    Persuasion (Charisma) 
    Art of convincing a creature in a friendly manner, to make certain decisions.  

    Programming (Intelligence) 
    Art of finding a vulnerability inside a system. Utilizing such vulnerability 
    could allow for a takeover of affected device. 

    Stealth (Dexterity) 
    Effectiveness of hiding, looking for cover, and covering tracks. 

    Swiftness (Dexterity) 
    Acrobatic and well though-though feats of speed, such as grabbing something quickly, running and attacking in disruptive conditions, falling down and landing of your feet or walking on a rope. 

    Reflex (Dexterity) 
    Avoidance of area effects and certain traps. 
    Requires Cunning I for proficiency, and Cunning IV for expertise. 

    Vehicle Usage (Dexterity or Intelligence) 
    A wide group of skills involved in using most types of vehicles, such 
    as driving off the road, setting up warp engines, or syncing array of weapons. 

    Willpower (Wisdom) 
    Avoidance of mind-affecting effects. 
    Requires Mysticism I for proficiency and Mysticism IV for expertise.
