### Spark Currency
    Elphar Sparks are tokens for energy exchange.
    One spark represents 10 Megajoules (2390 kcal) of energy.
    Rifle shells contain 1 Spark of explosive charge.
    Common coin for spark exchange, Solar Disc, can hold up to 5 Sparks.
    They can recharge is direct sunlight, and easily transfer stored energy
    via it's metal structure. One spark is relatively insignifact amount of money,
    however exchange of partialy charged Solar Discs happens often.

### Energy Containers for exchange.
    1 Solar:
        Solar coin, with capacity of up to 5 Sparks
    1 Core:
        Water bottle sized cylindrical device, able to contain up to 10000 Sparks.
    1 Rod:
        Fission rod made of radioactive alloy, calibrated for 1 Million Sparks
        Almost all nuclear reactors use the same exact form of fission rods.
    1 Antimatter Solar:
        Exotic form of energy store. Made of 1 gram of antimatter,
        contained within a superconductive crystal.
