### Special Weapons
    Kinetic Orb: 
        Self-propelled device, that user controls with their mind. It can strike targets directly in 15 meter range, using intelligence melee attacks. 
        Additionally, it can blast enemies at range with beams of energy. 
        Accuracy: intelligence modifier + proficiency 
        Damage: 1d6 + intelligence modifier of bludgeoning (Melee up to 15 meters) 
        Damage: 1d4 + intelligence modifier of lightning damage (Range up to 60 meters) 
        

    Ordinance Platform: 
        Armor attachment, that adds 2 weapon slots and 2 utility slots to medium or heavy plating. 
        Weapon slots can use spaceship grade weapons, with size damage multiplier of 1x. 


    Beartrap: (25 sparks) 
        Base DC: 10, reflex check 
        Damage: 3d4 piercing, always applies bleeding. 

    Stun Cloud Trap: (2500 sparks)
        Base DC: 14, constitution check 
        Damage: 4d6 poison, 10 meter radius
