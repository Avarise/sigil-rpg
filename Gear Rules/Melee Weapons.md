### Melee Weapons
    Dagger: 
    Accuracy: strength modifier + proficiency 
    Damage: 1d4 + strength modifier of piercing damage 
    Other:  
    --light (can be used with dexterity modifier instead of strength) 
    --bonus +1 Lethality (extra critical strikes) 

    Short Combat Blade: 
    Accuracy: strength modifier + proficiency 
    Damage: 1d6 + strength modifier of slashing damage 
    Other:  
    --light (can be used with dexterity modifier instead of strength) 

    Long Combat Blade: 
    Accuracy: strength modifier + proficiency 
    Damage: 1d8 + strength modifier of slashing damage 
    Other: 
    --versatile (can be use two handed, for 1d10 damage)
    
    Ripper: 
    This weapon represent various brutal weapons, like two handed axes, gigantic machetes, or weapons made from big beasts. 
    Accuracy: strength modifier + proficiency 
    Damage: 1d10 + strength modifier of slashing damage 
    Other:  
    --heavy (15 str required) 
    --versatile if user has more than 17 str (can be used two handed for 1d12) 

    Stun Rod: 
    Accuracy: strength modifier + proficiency 
    Damage: 1d6 + strength modifier of bludgeoning damage 
    Other: 
    --normal attacks also apply effects of expose attack     

    Polearm: 
    Accuracy: strength modifier + proficiency 
    Damage: 1d8 + strength modifier of bludgeoning damage 
    Other: 
    --versatile (can be use two handed, for 1d10 damage) 
    --can attack opponent entering melee range as reaction, if the user used Brace action. 

    Combat Shield: 
    Accuracy: strength modifier + proficiency 
    Damage: 1d4 + strength modifier 
    Other:  
    --thrown (10/30 meters) 
    --bash (expose attack knocks prone) 
    -- +2 bonus to AC while shield is equipped 

    Combat Wire: 
    Accuracy: strength modifier + proficiency 
    Damage: 1d4 + strength modifier 
    Other:  
    --light (can be used with dexterity modifier instead of strength) 
    --extra range (5 meters)
