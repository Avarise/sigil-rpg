### Item affixes

    Each item may have one suffix and many prefixes, for up to 5 total modifiers. 
    Prefixes and suffixes have different requirements such as crafting time, skills and materials needed. 
    Each existing modification increases base DC of a modification recipe by 4, as well as increases material cost by 50%.
