### Ranged Weapons:
    Basic Hand Gun 
    Accuracy    : dexterity modifier + proficiency modifier 
    Range       : 120/240 meters 
    Damage      : 1d6 + dexterity modifier, piercing or energy damage (depends on variant kinetic gun or blaster) 
    Reload      : 10 shot magazine, 1 Focus reload 
    Other       : - 

    Rotary Hand Gun: 
    Accuracy    : dexterity modifier + proficiency modifier 
    Range       : 60 meters 
    Damage      : 1d8 + dexterity modifier, piercing damage 
    Reload      : 6 bullet chamber, 1 Focus to reload 

    Kinetic Rifle: 
    Accuracy    : dexterity modifier + proficiency modifier 
    Range       : 240 meters 
    Damage      : 1d10 + dexterity modifier of piercing damage 
    Reload      : 6 bullet magazine, 1 Focus to reload 

    Burst Rifle:  
    Accuracy    : dexterity modifier + proficiency 
    Range       : 60 meters / 120 meters 
    Damage      : 1d6 + dexterity modifier of piercing damage  
    Reload      : 6 bursts, 1 Focus to reload 
    Other       : Deals damage in line to all targets in the primary range. 

    Plasma Rifle:     
    Accuracy    : dexterity modifier + proficiency  
    Range       : 60 meters 
    Damage      : 2d6 + dexterity modifier of lightning damage 
    Reload      : 6 charge capacitor, no reload, cannot use for one round while recharging 

    Mechanical Bow: 
    Accuracy    : dexterity modifier + proficiency modifier 
    Range       : 60/120 meters 
    Damage      : 1d8 + strength modifier of piercing damage 
    Reload      : As part of attack 
    Other       : +1 Bonus to Lethality 

    Plasma Bow:  
    Accuracy    : dexterity modifier + proficiency 
    Range       : 60 meters (bolt) 
    Damage      : 2d6 + strength modifier of lightning damage (bolt) 
    Cone DC     : 8 + dexterity modifier + proficiency 
    Range       : 10 meters (cone)        
    Damage      : 1d6 + strength modifier of lightning damage (cone)
