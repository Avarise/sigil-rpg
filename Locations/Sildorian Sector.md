### Sildoria

Surface of Sildoria Prime is covered with temples, cities, shipyards and other amenities. Highly active atmosphere combined with few planetary generators, create the thickest known energy shield around the planet, making it impossible for large and capital vessels to enter the atmosphere, although Sildorian made ships excel at this task. 

 

Many square miles were ruined, after devastating waves of Yuulithid armies from planet purgatory ravaged the planet. But Sildorians did not immediately rebuild their cities. Instead, they let nature overtake some of the ruins, creating beautiful landscape.
