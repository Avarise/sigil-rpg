### Arc Sector
    Solar system around a star Arc.
    Arc was a first Green Star of the Elphar Commonwealth.
    Elphs living in this sector tend to heavily modify their bodies,
    often integrating gear, such as combat blades, or plating.

### Local objects and zones:
    Green Star Arc

    Rocky planet Arc-8, with 

    Sigil Rock,
    the central hub of Arc-8. This sprawling fortress-city stands out from the photovoltaic fields of the planet.
    Originally terraformed to fuel powerful ritual of shared dream by an cryptic elph lord Emerald Shaper. 
    These days, the endless photovoltaic fields produce vast amount of energy, which is exchanged
    for imports of high quality resources.
    
    Government District:  
    Meticulously planned series of buildings that house the administrative functions of Arc-8.
    These sleek, towering structures are made shaded composites, self-cleaning,
    reflective surfaces that shimmer under the green star Arc's light. 
    Key buildings in this district include: 
        Council Hall: Where the leaders of Arc-8 convene to discuss policy and governance. This hall is equipped with advanced holo-conferencing systems and security measures.
        Judicial Tower: The center for legal proceedings and justice on Arc-8, featuring multiple courtrooms and detention facilities. 
        Administrative Hub: Houses the various bureaucratic offices that manage the day-to-day operations of the planet. 
 
    Spaceport and Particle Lance:
    Adjacent to the Government District is the bustling Spaceport, a vital artery for trade and travel.
    This facility is equipped with state-of-the-art docking bays, repair hangars, and customs checkpoints.
    Dominating the spaceport is the imposing Particle Lance, a massive surface-to-orbit gun. 
 
    Rejuvenation and Recreation Centers:
    Sigil Rock boasts numerous rejuvenation cabinets and spas,
    providing residents and visitors with cutting-edge bio-engineered treatments that promote health and longevity.
    These facilities offer a range of services, from simple relaxation therapies to advanced regenerative procedures. 
        Rejuvenation Cabinets: Private pods where individuals can undergo accelerated healing and anti-aging treatments. 
        Spas: Luxurious centers offering holistic wellness experiences, including massages, hydrotherapy, and aromatherapy. 

    Residential and Recreational Areas:
    The residential quarters of Sigil Rock are designed for comfort and efficiency,
    providing ample living space for its inhabitants.
    These quarters are complemented by a variety of recreational centers,
    ensuring a balanced lifestyle for all residents. 
        Sleeping Quarters: Modern, spacious apartments equipped with the latest amenities. 
        Recreation Centers: Facilities that include gyms, sports arenas, and entertainment hubs. 
        Parks: Bio-engineered flora and fauna create lush, green spaces that offer a serene escape from the urban environment. These parks are meticulously maintained and feature a variety of exotic plants and animals, providing both beauty and biodiversity. 

    Commercial District:
        A bustling area where vendors sell everything from advanced technology to artisanal goods.
        Besides that, numerous Corporate Offices including the headquarters of Arc Bio Engineering Lounge (ABEL),
        which provides life-support systems, nano-machine fluids, and other essential bio-tech products.
